Commitment to Quality

For each of my bracelets, I use the same high quality materials that I've been using ever since I started, including genuine Swarovski crystals, glass beads, and semiprecious gemstones. I also take special care in the packing and shipping of bracelets, so that you will receive them in perfect condition. A lot of love goes into each unique design and I hope that you'll enjoy wearing and sharing my bracelets.

About the Jewelry Designer

Jewelry designer, Deb Ruf, has been designing and crafting beautiful beaded bracelets for more than seven years. By keeping up with the latest in bead design and technology, and maintaining great relationships with bead providers, she is able to offer unique beaded bracelets at affordable prices. For Deb, helping women feel happy and beautiful is the best part of her business.

Story of this Site

Hi, my name is Deb Ruf and I'm the jewelry designer behind Bracelets by Deb. I started making beaded bracelets for family and friends more than five years ago. After my coworkers began asking me how they could purchase my beaded jewelry, my oldest son, Adam, suggested that a website would make it easy for people to order my bracelets. Lucky for me, he's a web designer and this site was my Christmas present for 2011. I'm always coming up with new designs, so bookmark my site and check back regularly to get an early look at new bracelet designs throughout the year.
