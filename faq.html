Frequently Asked Questions

Are your bracelets made to last?

Yes, I use only high-quality materials and if you take good care of your beaded bracelets, they should remain beautiful for many, many years.

How can I take care of my beautiful bracelets?

You can clean your bracelets with a soft, dry cloth (a free small cleaning cloth is included with every bracelet purchase). It is also safe to use a dampened cloth with a little non-abrasive soap and then clean the soap off with a rinsed-out dampened cloth. Never soak your bracelets or use harsh cleansers. If your bracelets' sterling silver tarnishes, you can use a cleaner specifically made for that purpose or a silver polishing cloth.

How much does shipping cost?

Shipping to addresses in the United States is free. For International Shipping costs, please send us a message with: 1) The names of any bracelets you want and 2) the quantity of bracelets you wish to purchase.

How can I pay for your bracelets?

You can use any credit card accepted by PayPal, or you can use your PayPal account.

Is it safe to purchase a bracelet from your website?

Yes, all transactions are securely processed directly by PayPal.

Will my bracelet arrive in perfect condition?

I want each bracelet to arrive as perfectly as if I've just handed it to you. To make sure all bracelets are protected during transit, I put each bracelet in its own protective storage bag and then wrap them with additional protective packaging material before placing them into the shipping container.

Do your bracelet designs come in other colors?

I've carefully chosen the colors for the designs to beautifully accessorize a wide range of your outfits. So for now, I'm not able to offer color variations for any designs.

How do you choose a name for each bracelet?

Sometimes a bracelet design will show off her personality and her name comes quite naturally. Other times, I check lists of names to find a good match.
